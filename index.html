Our Identity

Team Envision PNEC NUST formerly known as Team PNEC NUST was founded in 2009, almost a decade ago. A student led venture, engineering minds teamed up to design, fabricate and manufacture their innovate fuel efficient vehicles with the Shell Eco Marathon Asia (SEMA) international stage in mind.

Today, the team has grown ten fold and garnered a following of over thousands of people from Pakistan and abroad through its interactive online and physical communication campaigns and roadshows. The team's communication campaign timeline is always aligned to the cardinal themes of eco-friendliness, fuel efficiency and a green future in mind.
